ABOUT MORGAN INDUSTRIAL PARK 关于摩根工业园（奥贡州）

Who We Are 我们是谁

It is planned to build an industrial park with an area of over 3,000 acres in Ogun State in alongside the expressway in Ode-remo, Ogere and Iperu. The park will include various types of factories, trade malls, logistics parks, as well as a section of farmland within it.The park will focus on developing industries such as food, textiles, automobiles, home appliances, new energy, and agriculture. 计划在奥贡州Ode-remo、Ogere、Iperu的高速路边，建立一座面积超过3000Acres的工业园，包含各类型工厂、商贸城、物流园，并在其中做一部分农场。园区将重点发展食品、纺织、汽车、家电、新能源、农业等产业。

With a team of highly skilled professionals and cutting-edge equipment, we transform vision into reality through strategic partnerships and execution excellence. 凭借高技能的专业团队和先进的设备，我们通过战略合作伙伴关系和卓越的执行力将愿景转化为现实。
