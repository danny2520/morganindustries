About Us 关于我们

MORGAN MERCHANT INVESTMENT LTD is a comprehensive investment company headquartered in Nigeria. The company has a wide range of business operations, including but not limited to mining, agriculture (Nine Stars), industrial park development, manufacturing and other fields. It has made great contributions to the improvement of the local economy and provided a large number of jobs . 摩根招商投资有限公司是总部位于尼日利亚的综合性投资公司。公司业务覆盖广泛，包括但不限于矿产开发、农业（Nine Stars）、工业园开发、生产制造等领域，为当地经济的提升做出了极大的贡献，并提供了大量的工作岗位。
